Welcome to Mass Mail Dispatcher

A mass mail dispatcher is a tool or system used to send bulk emails to multiple recipients simultaneously. It is commonly used for announcements, updates, promotional campaigns, or notifications. The dispatcher ensures efficient delivery, allows customization of messages, and may include tracking features like open rates and click-through rates.
